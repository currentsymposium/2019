CURRENT is a multidisciplinary, intersectional, music and electronic art symposium supporting women and non-binary artists in Vancouver and beyond. For the 2019 iteration of CURRENT, we are launching a 3-day paid mentorship program (July 23-25) to support emerging and aspiring cultural producers, curators, and event organizers in Vancouver.

Selected participants will be paid $1,000 to participate in the 3 mandatory sessions. We highly encourage Indigenous folks, men of colour, queer and trans people to apply.

Applications close June 7th.

Program includes: grant writing, concept and curatorial vision development, community building, project planning, budgeting, leadership, networking, managing multiple stakeholders, multi-day event programming, logistics, etc.

Format will be talks, open discussions, workshops, site visits, individual consultations.

Participants will be offered future event planning opportunities where they can apply their skills through our partners at New Forms Festival, VIVO Media Arts Centre, and the Western Front.
